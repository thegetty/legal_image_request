    Image Specifications:

    Standard files provided are 300 ppi TIFF files for offset printing and 72 ppi JPEG low compression files for web and other electronic uses. All images will be in color unless photography is only available in black and white. Digital files may not be reduced by more than 60%. If a reduction of 60% or more is required, another smaller image must be requested. Images may not be enlarged. Rush handling is not available for all digital files.
