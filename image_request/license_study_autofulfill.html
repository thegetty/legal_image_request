    Request for Study, Reference, or Lecture Images for Personal Use

    Reproduction in a Print Publication, E-Publication, Electronic Media or Television/Film/Video Permission available via a different form.

    All requests for images and permission to reproduce are subject to approval. Each request is separately considered, and permission is granted on a case-by-case basis at the sole discretion of the Museum.

    Color images will be provided unless photography is only available in black and white. Files will be 72 PPI JPEG at 1000x1000 pixels, appropriate for PowerPoint use. Note that JPEG Files may not be re-used for print publication in the future.
